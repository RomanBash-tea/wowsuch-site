the mechanics we're testing

Seven ideas, still on the whiteboard

Seams

Neighbors in a Chain aren't always well matched — mismatched uptime or hosting leaks a slice of Energy at the Seam between them. Sync up and the leak shrinks; ignore it and you're closer to the worst case.

Tribes

Every Body lives in a physical container at the farm — that container is a Tribe. Tribes run weekly on real KPIs (uptime, efficiency), so there's bragging rights on the line too.

Watcher

Watcher is a role, not a job title — track your own Body's telemetry closely enough and you earn a bonus for it, plus the standing right to pull its real logs: restarts, hashrate drops, repairs.

Healing

Hardware ages honestly here. When a Body goes in for real repair, that's a Healing event — tracked on real telemetry, not glossed over like the rest of the category tends to.

Buyout

Want the whole Body? Buy it out — no Chain, no neighbors, just the minimum commission. Open slots afterward and you become the head, earning the reverse flow from whoever settles in below you.

Reincarnation

When a Body reaches end of life, its Entities carry their standing into a new Body of the same class. Die early instead, and the insurance reserve covers the move — no new payment.

Energy

Energy is what a Body actually mines — real LTC + DOGE. It flows down the Chain in settling order, split by position, Seams, and the commission each link pays along the way.
