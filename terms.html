1.The service

WowSuch sells, rents, hosts, and services the Bitmain Antminer L9 — a Scrypt ASIC that merge-mines Litecoin (LTC) and Dogecoin (DOGE) on the same electricity. We operate WowSuch Pool, which finds blocks and routes rewards, and WowSuch MPC, a non-custodial wallet in which your rewards are held under keys only you control. Machines are housed in a monitored United States facility. By using this site or placing an order, you agree to these terms.

You are responsible for ensuring that purchasing, renting, hosting, or operating mining hardware, and receiving cryptocurrency, is lawful where you live, and for any taxes that result. We may decline or cancel any order, and may require identity verification (KYC) before fiat cash-out or where required by law.

3.Orders & payment

An order on this site is a reservation. After you select a package and submit your details, we email a secure invoice with payment instructions, typically within the hour. Your order is confirmed once payment is received and cleared.

For an outright purchase, the price of an Antminer L9 is $4,995. A $100 deposit, which is non-refundable, reserves your selected machines; the balance is due within 30 days. Once paid, machines are racked in the hosting facility and begin mining under a one-year hosting contract. Fleets of up to 500 machines are supported.

You may pay your invoice by USDC or other supported crypto, by card, or by bank wire. Card payments are processed by a third-party, PCI-compliant payment processor; we never collect or store full card numbers on this site. Prices are in US dollars and exclusive of any taxes or duties that may apply to you.

4.Hosting terms

Hosting is a flat $0.079/kWh all-in, on a one-year term, in our United States facility, which is 24/7 monitored with an on-site repair team. Machines are live within one business day of cleared payment.

Mining runs on WowSuch Pool at a locked 3% pool fee. Hosting and the pool fee are withheld automatically from your daily rewards before payout. When the pool auto-withholds your hosting, the security deposit and one-time setup fee are waived; if you prefer to pay hosting yourself, a two-month deposit and a setup fee apply instead. To protect you, we curtail or self-mine rather than run your machine at a loss whenever mining revenue would fall below the $0.079/kWh hosting cost.

5.Rent-to-own terms

Rent-to-own is fractional, by the chip. A single Antminer L9 is 288 chips across four hashboards; you choose how many chips to rent, subject to a 12-chip minimum. Each chip is about $20.83, paid over 18 months at 0% interest (about $1.16 per chip per month, so roughly $13.89/mo at the 12-chip minimum) while it mines.

Repairs are included for the entire 18-month term at no extra cost. Payments are due regardless of mining results. At month 18, once your chips are paid off, you may (a) pay the difference to own the whole L9 — a $6,000 total basis; (b) walk away and stop paying; or (c) have the actual chip desoldered and shipped to you as a keepsake. A single chip cannot mine on its own.

6.Warranties & repairs

Outright purchases include a 90-day warranty plus 90 additional days of free repairs — six months of repair coverage in total — using original parts and our on-site technicians. Repairs are typically completed in under 14 business days, and each repair carries its own fresh 90-day warranty. On rent-to-own plans, repairs are included for the full 18-month term, during which cumulative repair downtime may reach up to three months. Except as stated here, hardware is provided as-is to the extent permitted by law.

7.Buyback & exit

There is no lock-in beyond your hosting term. To exit, give 90 days' notice. We will broker the sale of your hardware at fair market value, or derack and ship it to you for a $25 per-machine derack fee plus shipping. Any buyback or brokered price reflects live fair market value at the time of sale and is not a guaranteed amount.
